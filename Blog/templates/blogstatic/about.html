I am a Computer Science student of Nazarbayev University. I have been into the programming since the 2017. Initially I have started learning the Computer Science with simple C++ applications. After that, I switched to the Python in order to create my own web-site. After some practice I have created this Django based website. I didn't stop on that and created my Telegram application, which works with the cinema schedule. When I enrolled to the university, I started to dive into the Calculus and Computer Science. I got some skills to work with the C/C++. In my GitHub profile, you can find different algorithms and Data Structures which I have implemented during my education. For example: LinkedList-Based Stack, Binary Search Tree or HashTable. Apart from that I dedicated a lot of time reading such books as "Computer Networks" A.Tannenbaum, "Learning Python" M.Lutz and etc.

During my Foundation Program in my University, I successfully finished my English for Academic Purposes course, which received the accreditation from BALEAP and wrote some my own Research Projets. Furthermore, I have received some experience of working in the start-up project, which was connected to the face-recognition cameras. I had to design, produce and modify Printed Circuit Boards with the following their installation into the devices.

I am also in love with learning different languages. I am bilingual in Kazakh and Russian, since 2011 I have been learning English. Currently, I have a part time job as an English Language Teacher. In 2017 I started to learn German language and reached the C1.1 level (first part of the Advanced Level by CEFR scale). Finally, I have recently started learning Chinese language and I am going to reach the A2 level soon.
